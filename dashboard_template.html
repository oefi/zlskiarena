Methodology
Data sources, scoring formula, and honest caveats
Data Source

Primary: Open-Meteo ERA5-Land reanalysis (~9 km grid). Two queries per resort — base elevation and summit elevation — providing independent temperature, snowfall, wind and snowpack estimates at each altitude tier.

Cross-validation: Snow-Forecast.com historical reports (sparse), ZAMG climate normals.

Ski Quality Score

A composite 0–1 score built from four components:
snow 35% temperature 25% sunshine 25% wind −15%

Persona presets re-weight these components. Rain at base incurs an additional penalty. Powder days (≥10 cm fresh) add a 0.20 bonus.

Caveats

ERA5-Land is model reanalysis, not station observations. Micro-valley effects may be missed. 7 years is recent history, not climatology. 2026 is a partial season. Snowmaking coverage (30 km at Nauders) is not reflected in model data — poor-snow days may still be skiable on groomed pistes.
